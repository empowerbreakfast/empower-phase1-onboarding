What Is Phase One?

Phase One is the required first step for every new Area Director. You'll learn the emPower Breakfast mission, your role and benefits, the five components of a successful event, and the fundamentals of Sponsor recruiting.

Once you complete the training, review the study guide, and pass the assessment below, you'll have everything you need to begin recruiting your first two Sponsors — the final requirement before moving into full Area Director status and gaining access to the complete AD training library.
