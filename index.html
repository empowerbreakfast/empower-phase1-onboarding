Welcome to emPower Breakfast

Phase One is your opportunity to learn about emPower Breakfast and explore whether your local business community is a good fit for an event. It takes about an hour to complete the training — but the real work of Phase One happens in the conversations you have afterward.

Phase One — Explore & Learn → Recruit 2 Sponsors → Decision With Jim & Michael

What Is Phase One?

Phase One is the required first step for every prospective Area Director. You'll learn the emPower Breakfast mission, your role and benefits, the five components of a successful event, and the fundamentals of Sponsor recruiting.

Start by reading your Onboarding Kit below, then complete the training, review the study guide, and pass the assessment. But completing these steps is only part of Phase One.

Beyond the Training: What We're Really Asking of You

Phase One is also a real-world test of an idea: is your business community ready for an emPower Breakfast? During this phase, you'll be talking with business owners in your market, gauging genuine interest, and working to recruit your first two Sponsors.

This isn't just a checklist item — it's how we, and you, learn whether this is the right opportunity, in the right market, at the right time.

🎯 What Happens After You Recruit Your First Two Sponsors

Passing the assessment and recruiting two Sponsors does not automatically make you a full Area Director. Once those two Sponsors are in place, that decision is based on:

  • Your own willingness and enthusiasm to continue
  • Whether the business community in your market seems genuinely receptive to a new event
  • Jim and Michael's confidence in you as the right person to lead it

Phase One exists so that everyone — you included — can make a well-informed decision. Moving forward is a mutual choice, not an automatic next step.
